What does it do? Jarvis is equipped with an extensive array of features, empowering users to effortlessly transmit a diverse range of media formats such as images, audio tracks, TikTok videos, and screen captures of web pages. Its seamless integration with the Facebook Messenger platform ensures optimal accessibility and user convenience, providing a robust solution for multimedia sharing needs.

Jarvis operates within the dynamic framework of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, enriching its functionalities with the immersive backdrop of this iconic fictional universe.
